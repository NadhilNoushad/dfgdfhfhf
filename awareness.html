What is solar energy?

Solar energy is the radiant light and heat from the Sun that is harnessed using a range of ever-evolving technologies such as solar heating, photovoltaics, solar thermal energy, solar architecture, molten salt power plants, and artificial photosynthesis.

Benefits of solar energy

Solar energy is a renewable and sustainable source of energy that can help us reduce our reliance on fossil fuels. It is also a clean and non-polluting source of energy, which can help us improve air quality and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Getting started with solar energy

If you are interested in learning more about solar energy or getting started with your own solar project, there are a number of resources available to you. You can contact a local solar installer, visit the website of the Solar Energy Industries Association, or talk to your local government about solar incentives.
